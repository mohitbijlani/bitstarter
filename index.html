Something you need? MeWants lets you post tasks you'd like completed, as well as the price you're willing to pay for the service, to a willing audience in your area. Need cash in a flash? With MeWants, you can search a list of available tasks in your vicinity. Simply select and complete one, and the money will roll right in! Whether it's grocery pick-up or yardwork, help with moving, tutoring, or just help lining your pockets, MeWants is the quick, easy way to get what you want.

FAQ

Who is a TaskMaker?
Anyone who posts a task.
Who is a TaskMaster?
Anyone who bids on/completes a task.
What tasks can I get done on MeWants?
With MeWants, we want to create an ecosystem that's safe and appropriate for everyone to use. So, as long as your tasks aren't lewd or inappropriate, you can get just about anything done on MeWants.
How do I post a task?
Once you've downloaded the MeWants app and created your profile, simply choose 'Post a Task' on the Home page, fill in the relevant details, and hit 'Post'. That's it. Your task will now be visible to all TaskMasters who are within the distance specified by you while posting the task. Remember to be as detailed in your task description as possible - the more clarity TaskMasters have, the faster will your tasks get done!
How do I choose who gets to do my task?
To see all bids received for a task you've posted, click on 'My Tasks' on the Home page. Choose the task you're interested in under the 'Tasks I have posted' view (should be in blue if you've received at least one bid) You will now be taken to the 'Bids' screen where you will see all bids you've received for the task. You can also choose to sort these bids by TaskMaster rating, TaskMaster distance, or bid amount. To choose a particular TaskMaster, simply select the bid and click on 'Select' under the 'Bid Description' screen.
How do I edit a task I've posted?
To edit a task, choose the task from the 'Tasks I have posted' screen and click 'Edit' (top right) on the 'Bids' screen. Note that you can only increase the task distance if you do not want to repost the task as a new one, however changing any other task parameter(s) will result in a re-post of the task. Note: You can only edit a task before you have accepted a bid on it.
How do I pay for a task I've posted?
You can either use Cash, your Paypal account, or a credit/debit card to pay for your tasks. To use any payment method apart from cash you would require a paypal account.
When do I make a payment for a task I've posted?
When you confirm your selection of a TaskMaster, MeWants holds on to the accepted bid amount + a 7% service charge. Only once your task is completed by the TaskMaster and once you've marked the task as complete, will MeWants transfer the agreed bid amount to the TaskMaster. In case you choose cash, the transaction will be handled offline between the TaskMaster and you.
